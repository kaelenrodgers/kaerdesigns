Hello!

I’m Kaelen Rodgers, and I am a graphic designer and writer looking to gain more experience in typography and layouts. My creative experience has given me a deep love for studying the way that culture, literature, and art influence each other. I am comfortable in Adobe Suite, Microsoft Office. Thanks to being a quick learner, I’ve also been able to pick up photography and programing in html and css.

My freelance design work includes work for Taylor University’s Equestrian Team, Writing Center, and Honors Guild; Hugh O’Brian Youth Leadership; and Parnassus, Taylor University’s annual literary journal. There was a wide variety in these assignments from poster and stickers to book layout and t-shirt designs. My writing has been published in Aboite Independent, the Evangelical Church Library Association, Writer's Digest, and Parnassus.

I have also spent time in leadership as a college First Year Experience (FYE) group leader and then as a cabinet member in charge of the FYE group leaders. I am currently a tutor at my college's writing center and was recently requested to return as a FYE cabinet member.

When I’m not making art or writing, I love traveling. I have been all across the United States, lived in England for two years, and just spent a month studying culture in Asia.

I also love chocolate.
